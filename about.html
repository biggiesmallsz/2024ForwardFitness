Common Exercieses

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpee are a great, ful body exercies to increase your strength and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a dquat and extend forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted poistiom.
Plank
Panks build your core strength. To perform a pank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the posttion as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a goood cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor ina a push up position, then bring one knee up to your chest and then switch as quickly as you can(as though you are climbing a mountain).
